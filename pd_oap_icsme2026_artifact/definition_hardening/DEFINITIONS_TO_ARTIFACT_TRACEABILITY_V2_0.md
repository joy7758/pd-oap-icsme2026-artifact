# Definitions To Artifact Traceability v2.0

| Concept | Artifact representation | Current v1.x evidence | v2.0 needed extension | Reviewer concern addressed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| public-data operation | Operation type, data object reference, requester, actor, policy basis, purpose, decision, conditions, and evidence context in each fixture. | 8 fixtures; 3 valid; 5 invalid; schema pass 8/8. | Expand operation families across access, reuse, and review cases with about 45 fixtures. | Clarifies the unit of analysis and avoids treating the work as a general public-data platform. |
| operation-accountability statement | Portable JSON statement plus validation and non-claim fields that bind operation, policy, provenance, evidence, and decision context. | reference checker matched 8/8; independent auditor matched 8/8; artifact runner `overall_passed true`. | Add completeness criteria, reference-closure checks, and fixture metadata for expected statement obligations. | Shows that the contribution is not merely a schema or log format. |
| structural inspectability | Required fields, reference closure, policy/evidence linkage, validation expectations, checker report, auditor report, and non-claims. | schema pass 8/8; reference checker matched 8/8; independent auditor matched 8/8. | Expand inspectability checks over larger fixture families and mutation-derived negative controls. | Separates artifact inspectability from legal or institutional approval. |
| reviewability | Same-case comparison criteria and reviewer-facing reconstruction of operation context. | same-case comparison ratios: `logs_only 0.2679`, `policy_only 0.1964`, `provenance_only 0.4196`, `full_pd_oap 1.0`. | Add raw counts, criteria totals, and possibly a small reviewer-task study after fixture expansion. | Explains what a reviewer can reconstruct and why ratios are not free-floating claims. |
| semantic failure surface | Expected failure codes, invalid fixtures, checker findings, auditor findings, and disagreement records. | 5 invalid fixtures; cross-checker agreement 8/8; `disagreement_count 0`. | Expand from 5 failure classes to at least 12 and add mutation evaluation. | Demonstrates that validation goes beyond JSON shape. |
| controlled fixture benchmark | Valid/invalid fixture suite, fixture inventory, generated reports, agreement matrix, and same-case comparison. | 8 fixtures; 3 valid; 5 invalid; schema pass 8/8; reference checker matched 8/8; independent auditor matched 8/8; cross-checker agreement 8/8; artifact runner `overall_passed true`. | Expand toward about 45 fixtures, at least 12 failure classes, failure injection, and raw-count reporting. | Answers the concern that v1.x looked like a small artifact sanity check rather than journal-scale evaluation. |
