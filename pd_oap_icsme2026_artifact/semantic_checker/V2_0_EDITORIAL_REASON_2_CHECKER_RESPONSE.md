# v2.0 Checker Response To Editorial Reason 2

## Editorial Concern

The evaluation appeared insufficient for TOSEM, and Section 9 was too brief to provide adequate empirical validation.

## Checker Contribution

The semantic checker matrix turns the 45 controlled fixtures into executable evidence. It reports whether valid, invalid, and edge fixtures match expected semantic outcomes across three operation families and 12 failure classes.

## What This Adds

- A 45-fixture checker matrix.
- Expected versus observed semantic outcomes.
- Failure-class coverage over controlled invalid fixtures.
- Operation-family counts.
- A reusable evaluation report for the v2.0 manuscript.

## Remaining Need

The checker matrix is necessary but not sufficient. Mutation evaluation is also needed to show controlled negative-control robustness over valid fixtures.
