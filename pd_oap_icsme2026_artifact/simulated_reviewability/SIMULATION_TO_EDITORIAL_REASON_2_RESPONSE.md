# Simulation to Editorial Reason 2 Response

The editorial criticism was that the evaluation appeared insufficient and that Section 9 was very brief.

The strict simulated reviewability evaluation adds:

- 117 source specimens
- 468 simulated review tasks
- representation ablation across logs_only, policy_only, provenance_only, and full_pd_oap
- deterministic scoring over six reviewability dimensions

It does not replace:

- semantic checker matrix
- mutation evaluation

It does not claim:

- human reviewer performance
- legal compliance
- deployment readiness

The simulation strengthens the evaluation by testing representation-level information availability in a reproducible way.
